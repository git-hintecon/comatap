About COMATAP!

COMATAP is a web-based application that helps concrete professionals with a simplified real-time wireless monitoring of concrete maturity and strength prediction. concrete asset managers and consultants can now track the progress of their concrete structures' maturity anywhere in the world. Thereby making their work much more integrated.

It also serves users latest updates about concrete structures.


About Maturity Test

Maturity of concrete has been around for decades, beginning from around the 1950s. In the 1970s, a string of fatal construction disasters motivated engineers and researchers to refine the technique for routine construction. Implementing the maturity method helps construction teams know when their concrete has reached adequate strength, sequence construction operations, and build concrete products safely and cost-efficiently.
